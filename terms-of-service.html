Frequently asked Questions

Are you a licensed company?

Yes, we are an officially licensed Holiday Home Operator Company regulated by the Dubai government and the Dubai Economy and Tourism Department (DTCM). You can trust InzziSuites to operate with the highest standards of professionalism and legality.

How do you ensure the property is kept in perfect condition?

We have a routine maintenance schedule and employ professional cleaners to keep the property in pristine condition. Additionally, we conduct regular inspections to address any issues promptly.

Who are the tenants that stay in the properties?

We cater to high-quality clients, including corporate travelers and short-term guests, ensuring that your property is occupied by responsible and respectful tenants.

How does the fixed rent guarantee work?

We offer a fixed rent guarantee, meaning you receive a consistent rental income regardless of occupancy. This removes the uncertainty of fluctuating rental income and ensures financial stability.

How does subletting to InzziSuites benefit me as a landlord?

Subletting to InzziSuites guarantees you a fixed rent, eliminating concerns about vacancy and rent collection. We also take care of property maintenance, ensuring it remains in top condition.
